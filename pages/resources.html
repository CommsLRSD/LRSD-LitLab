Resources

Helpful resources for implementing literacy interventions and understanding reading development.

Assessment Resources

  • DIBELS (University of Oregon): https://dibels.uoregon.edu/ - Official DIBELS website with training materials and resources
  • FastBridge Learning: https://www.fastbridge.org/ - Comprehensive assessment and intervention system
  • AIMSweb: https://www.aimsweb.com/ - Curriculum-based measurement and data management

Intervention Programs and Materials

Phonemic Awareness

  • Phonemic Awareness in Young Children: Research-based activities by Adams, Foorman, Lundberg, and Beeler
  • Road to the Code: Phonological awareness program by Blachman et al.
  • Equipped for Reading Success: Multisensory activities by Kilpatrick

Phonics and Decoding

  • Fundations: Wilson Language Training structured literacy program
  • Really Great Reading: Comprehensive phonics programs including Blast and Countdown
  • 95 Percent Group: Phonics and decoding interventions
  • UFLI Foundations: Free structured literacy curriculum from University of Florida

Fluency

  • Read Naturally: Evidence-based fluency intervention
  • Repeated Reading protocols: Free resources available from Reading Rockets
  • Quick Reads: Short passages for fluency practice

Comprehension and Vocabulary

  • Project CRISS: Strategic reading strategies
  • Bringing Words to Life: Vocabulary instruction by Beck, McKeown, and Kucan
  • Question-Answer Relationships (QAR): Comprehension strategy framework
  • Collaborative Strategic Reading (CSR): Comprehension strategies for groups

Professional Learning Resources

Organizations

  • International Dyslexia Association (IDA): https://dyslexiaida.org/ - Resources on structured literacy and dyslexia
  • Reading Rockets: https://www.readingrockets.org/ - Free resources for teaching reading
  • Center on MTSS: https://mtss4success.org/ - Resources on implementing MTSS
  • What Works Clearinghouse: https://ies.ed.gov/ncee/wwc/ - Reviews of research on educational interventions

Key Research and Publications

  • "Equipped for Reading Success" by David Kilpatrick - Understanding phonological awareness and orthographic mapping
  • "Reading in the Brain" by Stanislas Dehaene - Neuroscience of reading
  • "Scarborough's Reading Rope" - Framework for understanding skilled reading components
  • National Reading Panel Report - Research synthesis on effective reading instruction
  • Simple View of Reading (Gough & Tunmer) - Foundational reading comprehension model

Free Online Resources

  • Florida Center for Reading Research (FCRR): Free downloadable activities for literacy centers
  • Meadows Center: Free intervention activities and resources
  • Intervention Central: Free tools for progress monitoring and interventions
  • UFLI: Free structured literacy curriculum and resources
  • University of Oregon Big Ideas in Beginning Reading: Research summaries and activities

Technology Resources

  • Lexia: Adaptive literacy software
  • iReady Reading: Personalized instruction and assessment
  • Reading Horizons: Software for phonics and decoding
  • Raz-Kids: Leveled reading practice online
  • Nessy: Multisensory reading and spelling programs

Data Analysis Tools

  • Intervention Central: Free graphing and progress monitoring tools
  • STEEP (System to Enhance Educational Performance): Formative assessment decision-making tool
  • AIMLINE Calculator: Tool for setting realistic growth goals

Materials for Interventions

Suggested Manipulatives and Supplies

  • Letter cards (uppercase and lowercase)
  • Sound cards with keywords
  • Elkonin boxes (sound boxes) laminated or in sheet protectors
  • Counters or tokens
  • Dry erase boards and markers
  • Timers
  • Decodable texts at various levels
  • Word lists organized by phonics patterns
  • Fluency passages at various levels
  • Progress monitoring graphs
