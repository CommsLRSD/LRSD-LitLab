Multi-Tiered System of Supports (MTSS) is a framework for providing academic and behavioral support to all students. It uses data to identify students who need additional support and provides increasingly intensive interventions based on their needs.

Interventions should be implemented with fidelity for 6-8 weeks before making decisions about effectiveness. Session duration varies by tier: Tier 2 typically 20-30 minutes, Tier 3 typically 30-60 minutes per session.

Tier 2: Small groups of 3-6 students with similar needs. Tier 3: Very small groups of 1-3 students or one-on-one instruction for intensive support.
