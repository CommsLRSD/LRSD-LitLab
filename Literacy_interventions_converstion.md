# TIER 1

* **Flow Chart**  
*   
* Principles of Explicit and Systematic Instruction  
  * Read and check all 8 checkboxes  
    * Administer Literacy Screener  
      * Input Screener Results  
        * Instruction Effective (Subtest result Blue or Green)  
          * Success\! Continue and monitor with general curriculum.  
        * Instruction Ineffective (Subtest result Yellow or Red)  
          * Instruction Ineffective for 20% or more of students.  
            * Reteach General Curriculum: Consider areas of weakness discovered via Literacy Screener. Find evidence-based resources on the Interventions Menu. (Interventions Menu needs to be accessible here w/ Tier 1 filter applied as default.)  
          * Instruction Ineffective for fewer than 20% students.   
            * Move to Tier 2: Small Group Interventions  
* 

# TIER 2

   
**Flow Chart**  
*   
* Principles of Explicit and Systematic Instruction  
  * Read and check all 5 checkboxes  
    * Administer Drill Down Assessment: Use the menu to find and administer a drill down assessment that aligns with the needs of your students, as determined by the literacy screener. (Interventions menu available here w/ Tier 2 and Drill Down Assessments applied as default filter)  
      * 8-week Intervention: Use the Interventions menu to find an appropriate intervention and monitor student response with progress monitoring tools (as required). Administer for an 8-week period.  (Interventions menu available here w/ Tier 2 and Interventions applied as default filter)  
        * Progress Monitoring: After the 8-week period, administer the regularly scheduled progress monitoring literacy screener (DIBELS, CTOPP-2, THaFoL, IDAPEL)  
          * Instruction Effective (Subtest result Blue or Green)  
            * Success! Consider fading supports to Tier 1 and monitor  
          * Instruction Ineffective (Subtest result Yellow or Red)  
            * Administer Drill Down Assessment: Use the menu to find and administer a drill down assessment that aligns with the needs of your students, as determined by the literacy screener.  (Interventions menu available here w/ Tier 2 and Drill Down Assessments applied as default filter)  
              * 8-week Intervention: Use the Interventions menu to find an appropriate intervention and monitor student response with progress monitoring tools (as required).  
                * Progress Monitoring: After the 8-week period, administer the regularly scheduled progress monitoring literacy screener (DIBELS, CTOPP-2, THaFoL, IDAPEL) 
		  * Instruction Effective (Subtest result Blue or Green)  
           	    * Success! Consider fading supports to Tier 1 and monitor  
         	  * Instruction Ineffective (Subtest result Yellow or Red)
		    * Move to Tier 3: If student does not make expected progress in Tier 2 following two 8-week intervention cycles, tehy move into Tier 3. Fewere tan 10% of students should need ot be in Tier 3.  

# TIER 3

   
**Flow Chart** 
* Principles of Explicit and Systematic Instruction: Click Begin Tier 3
  * Administer Drill Down Assessment: Use the menu to find and administer a drill down assessment that aligns with the needs of your students, as determined by the literacy screener. (Interventions menu available here w/ Tier 3 and Drill Down Assessments applied as default filter)
    * 8-week Intervention: Use the Interventions menu to find an appropriate intervention and monitor student response with progress monitoring tools. Monitor student response to intervention weekly.  (Interventions menu available here w/ Tier 3 and Interventions applied as default filter) 
      * Progress Monitoring: After the 8-week period, administer the regularly scheduled progress monitoring literacy screener (DIBELS, CTOPP-2, THaFoL, IDAPEL) 
          * Instruction Effective (Subtest result Blue or Green)  
            * Success! Consider fading supports to Tier 1 and monitor  
          * Instruction Ineffective (Subtest result Yellow or Red) 
            * Meet with Clinicians: Meet with the appropriate Clinicians to discuss the next steps. (Link to Clinician contact info.)
